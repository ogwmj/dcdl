Strategy Overview

This team's entire strategy revolves around one core mechanic: generating Krypton Energy as quickly as possible. By having four "Superman Family" members use their ultimate abilities, the team unlocks one of the most powerful stacking Attack buffs in the game, turning the family into an unstoppable offensive force.

Key Synergy: Superman Family

Effect: Superman Family members generate 1 point of Krypton Energy for every cast Ultimate.

This energy powers up the entire family based on accumulated points:

  • LVL 1 (4 Krypton Energy): +25% ATK for all Superman Family allies.
  • LVL 2 (12 Krypton Energy): +50% ATK for all Superman Family allies.
  • LVL 3 (27 Krypton Energy): +75% ATK for all Superman Family allies.

Role & Skill Analysis

Warrior: Superman

The team's primary damage dealer and a core energy generator. His goal is to use his ultimate as often as possible to build Krypton Energy and benefit from the massive ATK buffs.

Under a Yellow Sun: Superman soars into space, gaining 1 permanent stack of [Solar Energy] before dealing Physical DMG to an enemy.
Heat Vision: Superman shoots a heat laser, dealing Physical DMG to an enemy.
Freeze Breath: Superman inhales deeply before exhaling a freezing breath, dealing Physical DMG to an enemy and [Freezing] them.
Man of Steel: Superman's DEF increases. At the start of battle, Superman gains 1 stack of ATK Up for each [Superman Family] ally present.

Warrior: Supergirl

A secondary damage dealer who becomes incredibly powerful once Krypton Energy levels are high. She is often the primary target for Superboy's buffs.

Solar Battery: Supergirl channels her power, dealing Physical DMG to enemies in a circular area. At [Krypton Energy] Lv. 1, she absorbs solar energy increasing this skill's Crit Rate. At [Krypton Energy] Lv. 3, solar energy enhances her might even more, increasing this skill's DMG to a staggering 2000% of her ATK.
Artic Breath: Supergirl draws in freezing air and exhales an icy blast, dealing Physical DMG to enemies in a fan-shaped area.
Family Ties: Increases ATK. At the start of battle, for each [Superman Family] ally present, Supergirl's Ultimate DMG is increased, stacking up to 5 time(s).

Supporter: Superboy

The key offensive buffer. His ultimate directly amplifies the team's damage output, creating a snowball effect as Krypton Energy builds.

Super Bro: Conner Kent unleashes his telekinetic power, increasing ATK for both himself and the ally with the highest ATK (Supergirl takes priority if present). This effect is capped at a maximum of 133% of the target's base ATK each time, stacks up to 3 time(s), and lasts until the end of battle. The ally also becomes immune to control effects 1 time. The ATK bonus is doubled for [Superman Family] allies.
Chill: Conner Kent exhales a blast of icy wind, dealing Physical DMG to enemies in a circular area and reducing their Physical DEF until the end of battle, stacking up to 4 time(s).
In the Genes: At the start of battle, Connor Kent's ATK increases for each level of [Krypton Energy].

Supporter: Krypto

The defensive backbone and passive buffer of the team. He provides crucial survivability and consistent stat increases.

Zoomies: Krypto zips around excitedly, knocking down enemies in his path and reducing their ATK. He also heals allies in his path. Krypto also passively increases his Energy and increases his healing effect.
Good Boy!: Krypto heals allies within range.
Cuddle Charge: Increases [Superman Family] Champions' ATK and DEF.

Supporter: The Flash

The accelerator. The Flash's entire purpose on this team is to jump-start the energy generation process by feeding his energy directly to the most important family member.

Speed Force Sharing: The Flash channels his Energy to an ally until they are fully charged or his Energy is depleted, and increases the target's ATK. Casting the Ultimate costs The Flash a percentage of Energy. In auto mode, the skill targets the ally with the highest ATK.
What a Rush: The Flash zips to the side of an ally to assist, increasing the target's ATK. In auto mode, the skill targets the ally with the highest ATK.
Speed Strike: The Flash attacks enemies while running in circles, dealing Physical DMG to enemies within a circular area.
