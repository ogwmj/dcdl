Your Daily Freebie Arsenal

These rewards are the bedrock of your early game progression. Don't leave them on the table!

In-Game Store Free Packs

The in-game store offers free resource packs that refresh every 8-12 hours. This is a simple, consistent source of bonus energy keys, experience, and currency. Make it a habit to check the store at least twice a day.

7-Day Login Hero

For new players, the first week is critical. By logging in for seven consecutive days, you are guaranteed to receive a free Mythic Champion, like Harley Quinn. This is a massive boost to any starting lineup.

From Trickle to Flood: Compounding Your Resources

Based on the income models in our Store Analyzer, here's how your daily efforts add up.

Gem Accumulation

Even at a "Low" activity level, you're earning approximately 130 gems daily and 880 weekly from various activities. Over a month, that's over 4,500 gems without spending a dime!

Anvil Income

Similarly, a "Medium" activity level generates around 30 anvils daily and 25 weekly. This steady stream is vital for upgrading your gear and pushing through tougher content.

This consistent income is what powers the Anvil Acquisition Planner. The more you log in, the more ambitious your upgrade plans can be!
