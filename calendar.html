DC: Dark Legion Limited Mythic Rotation Guide

Welcome to the unofficial Bleed Calendar for DC: Dark Legion. This tool tracks the "Bleed" recruitment events where Limited Mythic champions become available for a limited time. Because different servers operate on different schedules based on their launch date, it is crucial to check your specific Server Group.

Currently tracking rotations for: Earths 206-340, Earths 341-361, Earths 362-371, Earths 372-385, Earths 386+. Use this horizontal timeline to predict when champions like The Joker, Superman, and others will return. Click on any portrait to view the full card art and synergy details.
